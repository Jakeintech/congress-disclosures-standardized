Transparency metrics for PDF submission quality - identifies members submitting hard-to-process image-based PDFs

What This Shows
Members can submit disclosures as text-based PDFs (easy to extract data from) or image-based PDFs (scanned documents requiring OCR). High percentages of image PDFs may indicate submission practices that make transparency harder. This data is objective and factual - we report what we observe.
